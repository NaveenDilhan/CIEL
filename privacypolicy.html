We Value Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Buyer Information Collected:

  • Identity data: Name, gender, profile picture, date of birth
  • Contact data: Billing address, delivery address/location, email address, phone numbers
  • Biometric data: Voice files, facial recognition (for voice search and site usage)
  • Billing account information: Bank account details, credit card details, payment information
  • Transaction records/data: Order details, payment history, user clicks, product/service details
  • Technical data: IP address, login data, browser type/version, device information, cookies
  • Profile data: Username, password, account settings, order history, user preferences, feedback
  • Usage data: Site usage details, time spent, items searched, access times/dates, browsing history
  • Location data: Geographic location when shared (e.g., for location-based services)
  • Marketing and communications data: Marketing preferences, communication history, chat/email/call logs
  • Additional information: Government-issued ID copies for identity verification, as required

Seller Information Collected:

  • Identity and contact data: Name, date of birth/incorporation, company details, business-related information
  • Account data: Bank account details, credit card details, payment information
  • Transaction data: Order details, payment history, product/service details
  • Technical data: IP address, login data, browser type/version, device information, cookies
  • Profile data: Username, password, order history, user preferences, feedback
  • Usage data: Site usage details, time spent, items searched, access times/dates, browsing history
  • Location data: Geographic location when shared (e.g., for location-based services)
  • Marketing and communications data: Marketing preferences, communication history, chat/email/call logs
  • Additional information: Government-issued ID copies for authentication, if required

Information Usage - Buyers:

  1. Processing Orders:
    • Processing orders submitted through the Site
    • Delivering purchased products through third-party logistics partners
    • Updating on product delivery status
    • Providing customer support for orders
    • Verifying and processing payment transactions
  2. Providing Services:
    • Facilitating use of services and Site access
    • Administering buyer accounts
    • Displaying user information on the Site
    • Responding to queries, feedback, and disputes
  3. Marketing and Advertising:
    • Sending requested information and marketing materials
    • Conducting marketing and advertising activities
  4. Legal and Operational Purposes:
    • Identity verification for fraud detection
    • Ensuring data accuracy via third-party verification
    • Handling complaints and feedback
    • Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
  5. Analytics, Research, Business, and Development:
    • Understanding user experience and behavior
    • Improving site content and layout
    • Conducting surveys and research
    • Developing products and services
  6. Other Purposes:
    • Automated decision-making processes with user consent

Information Usage - Sellers:

  1. Providing Services:
    • Facilitating use of the Site for sellers
    • Shipping and delivering listed products via logistics partners
    • Responding to seller queries and disputes
    • Verifying seller documentation for onboarding
  2. Marketing and Advertising:
    • Sending marketing materials about products and services
  3. Legal and Operational Purposes:
    • Producing statistics and research
    • Ensuring compliance with legal requirements
    • Handling complaints and content takedowns
  4. Analytics, Research, Business, and Development:
    • Auditing data downloads
    • Understanding user experience
    • Improving site content and functionality
    • Developing technology and services
  5. Other Purposes:
    • Conducting automated decision-making processes

General Information Usage:

  • Account Administration: Managing accounts, financial transactions, and data downloads
  • Communication Preferences: Sending newsletters and marketing communications
  • Third-Party Sharing: Passing information to affiliates, agents, and service providers
  • Cookies: Use of cookies for site functionality and analytics
  • Security: Implementing measures to protect data security
  • User Rights: Accessing, correcting, and deleting personal data, and opting out of marketing communications
  • Minors: Not selling products to minors; parental consent for minor account use
